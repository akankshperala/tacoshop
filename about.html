About LTS

The Little Taco Shop (LTS) was established in 2022 with the vision of creating a warm and inviting space where people could gather to enjoy authentic, handcrafted tacos. Our shop was built from a deep love for delicious food, community, and the joy of sharing great flavors. Nestled in the heart of town, LTS offers a cozy and vibrant atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for taco lovers to indulge in fresh, flavorful dishes. We take pride in using high-quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a passion for exceptional service to bring a unique dining experience to our customers. Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United Stated?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
